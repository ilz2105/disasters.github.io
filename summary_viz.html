This scatterplot shows the average temperature (F) from 1953 to 2018, by region. Overall, the southeast region has the highest average temperatures and the Northeast has the lowest average temperatures.Though it may be hard to see, the average temperature increased from 70.81F to 72.28F from 1953 to 2018 in Florida, 59.25F to 62.26F in Arizona, 56.63F to 55.23F in Missouri, and 53.54F to 54.14F in New Jersey.
